About My City

Prayagraj, formerly known as Allahabad, is a historic and sacred city located in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh.
It is situated at the confluence of three holy rivers: Ganga, Yamuna, and the mythical Saraswati, making it one of the most important pilgrimage sites in Hinduism.
The city is famous for hosting the Kumbh Mela, the largest religious gathering in the world. Prayagraj is also known for its rich cultural heritage, ancient temples, and prestigious educational institutions like Allahabad University.
